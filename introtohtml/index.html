Did Dinosaurs Have Fur?

A newly identified species of dinosaur had some unusual features, including a long mane of fur down its back and stiff rod-like structures projecting from its shoulders. The dinosaur lived around 110 million years ago, during the Cretaceous period. Dino Frey at the State Museum of Natural History in Karlsruhe, Germany, and his colleagues discovered the species while examining slabs of limestone from north-eastern Brazil. They took high resolution X-ray images of the slabs, which revealed the torso of an unknown species of dinosaur, measuring approximately 50 centimetres long.
